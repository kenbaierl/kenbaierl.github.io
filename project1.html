Line Following Robot

This line-following robot uses 8 AA batteries in series to output 12 volts into a switch that powers two infrared sensors passed through a NOT gate to invert the signal and connect to two NMOS transistors that act as a voltage regulator for the motors. One difficulty in this project was that the initial PCB was accidentally printed inverted which stopped the circuit from working. Another challenge was that the wheels were moving too fast because of the voltage which led to us connecting a resistor between the battery pack and the switch.
